What Is GeoSafe?…

GeoSafe is an all-in-one insurance and risk protection solution for geospatial professionals across surveying, engineering, construction, planning, and GIS.

Policies Designed for You:

Equipment Insurance

Covers all your tools — from drones and RTK GNSS to dumpy levels & total stations:

  • Theft in the field or office

  • Damage from accidents or harsh field conditions.
  • Calibration and maintenance coverage to ensure data accuracy.

Liability Insurance

Protects against legal claims & laawsuites of professional or on-site incidents in case of:

  • Injury or accident liability for staff or third parties

  • Negligence coverage for mapping, data, or reporting errors

GeoSafe Protection Plan

A premium add-on designed for high-risk or remote operations: Each package is designed to communicate a sense of safety, preparedness & proffesional protection that is appealing to geospatial professionals working in high-risk environments.

 

  • Security escort services while conducting field operations for dangerous zones
  • Emergency response planning for field incidents
  • Evacuation protocols for teams in hostile environments
  • Ideal for surveyors, geologists, drone operators, and infrastructure field teams

Why It Matters:

Geospatial work often takes you off the grid; across forests, remote sites, urban boundaries, and disputed zones. GeoSafe helps you: Safeguard tools critical to your work, Avoid costly lawsuits or disruptions, Maintain continuity & credibility in your services. Whether you're mapping roads, minerals, land parcels, or utilities — protect your people, equipment, and reputation.
